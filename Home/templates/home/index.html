Overview

TeachersAnywhere is a non-profit website made for students by students. It aims to help students connect with tution centres and teachers all across Delhi. It was developed by Ayush Bansal (a class 12th student) in year 2020. With an easy to use interface, students can access to records about the tuition centres for Free. TeachersAnywhere also provides option to choose different classes and area to customise and further simplify their search. Our marketing manager Ashish Jindal is looking forward to expand our reach so as to provide students with a wide range of options.

Motivation

The motivation for this website comes from our struggles to find good tuition centres in class 11 and 12th. With no formal records available about the tution centres we had to rely only on word-of mouth to find about teachers. Finding good teacher can make or break your result. Our website provides you with many options so that you can make a better choice.
